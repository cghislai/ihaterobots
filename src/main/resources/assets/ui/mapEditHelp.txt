Here are the basic controls of the map editor:

- mouse or up/left/right/down move cursor
- keep left shift pressed and move cursor to select a rectangle
- press left mouse button or enter to put tile on selection
- press right mouse button or backspace to remove tiles
-- tiles will be removed sequentially on button press, 
    starting from front to back

- press C to clear map
- press P to place player
- press D to place door
